Speed Leaderboard

An Android app to compete with your friends.

Features of the app

Icon

Create your own personal account

Select the username field and choose a name when you first open the app.

Icon

Make groups with your friends

Either enter a premade group name or create your own.

Icon

Record highest driving speed

Enter driving mode to record your high speed on every road.

Icon

View fellow drivers speed within your group

Show group leaderboards and sort by speed, name, and street.

Icon

Create time trials

Drive a route, select a start and end location, and name your trial.

Icon

Compete against others on trials

View high scores on created time trials and compete to get the top speed.

Disclosure

This app uses a modified version of the Volley library, as well as the Material 2 and OSMDroid libraries.
